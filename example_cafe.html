A community cafe serving home cooked,locally sourced,organic food


With stunning views of the ocean,Example cafe offers the perfect environment unwind and recharge the batteries.

Our menu offers a wide range of breakfasts,brunches and lunches,including a range of vegetarian options.

Whether you sip on a fresh,hot coffee or a cooling smoothe ,you never need to feel rushed-relax with friends or just watch the world go by.
